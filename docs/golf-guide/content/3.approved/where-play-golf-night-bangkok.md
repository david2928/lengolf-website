# Where to Play Golf in Bangkok at Night

**Outdoor night golf on a full course in Bangkok is not a realistic option. Almost every course in and around the city closes to new tee times at or before sunset — typically 6:00–6:30 pm. If you want to play golf after dark in Bangkok, an indoor simulator is the practical answer.**

This guide explains why outdoor options are so limited, what evening driving range facilities exist, and where to find a proper evening golf session in the city.

---

## 1. Why Outdoor Bangkok Courses Do Not Offer Night Golf

Bangkok golf courses generally set their last tee time so that players can finish 18 holes before darkness. Given Thailand's latitude, sunset falls consistently between 6:00 and 6:45 pm throughout the year — there is no long summer evening here.

Running floodlit fairways on a full-length course requires significant infrastructure investment: poles, high-intensity lighting across 60–80 hectares, and ongoing electricity costs. No Bangkok-area course currently operates floodlit 18-hole rounds as a regular product. This is not unique to Bangkok — night golf on full courses is uncommon across Southeast Asia for the same practical reasons.

In short: if you arrive at a Bangkok course after 5:30 pm expecting to tee off a full round, you will almost certainly be turned away.

For context on the best times of day to book a round, see [Best Time of Day to Play Golf in Bangkok](/faq/best-time-of-day-golf-bangkok).

## 2. Evening Driving Ranges — What Exists

A handful of driving ranges in Bangkok do stay open into the evening, typically until 9:00–10:00 pm. These are useful for a warm-up session or practicing your swing, but they are not a substitute for a round of golf.

What to expect at a Bangkok evening driving range:

1. **Floodlit hitting bays** — standard multi-storey range format, often busy on weekday evenings
2. **Limited game feedback** — most outdoor ranges have basic target markers, no shot data
3. **No course simulation** — you are hitting balls into a lit range, not playing holes
4. **Weather dependency** — Bangkok's rainy season (May–October) can make outdoor ranges unusable in the evenings without notice

Ranges near central Bangkok tend to fill up quickly after office hours. Expect to wait for a bay during peak evening times, and bring your own balls or purchase buckets on-site.
