# LENGOLF SEO Guidelines for Golf Guide Content
*Use these rules when evaluating articles for SEO quality*

## URL Patterns
- `/guide/[slug]` → explainer/long-form guide content → lands in `data/explainer-pages.ts`
- `/faq/[slug]` → question-and-answer format → lands in `data/faq-pages.ts`
- `/cost/[slug]` → price/cost comparison content → lands in `data/price-guide-pages.ts`

## Title Guidelines
- Include primary keyword naturally in title
- Titles should be 50–65 characters
- Avoid clickbait; be specific and informative
- Thai/Bangkok specificity preferred over generic (e.g. "golf in Bangkok" > "golf in Asia")

## Meta Description Guidelines
- 140–160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Include a clear value proposition or what the reader will learn
- Mention Bangkok/Thailand where relevant for local SEO signal

## Content Structure Requirements
- Must have a clear intro paragraph (2–4 sentences max) that states what the article covers
- Must use H2 headings for major sections
- Each section should be 150–400 words
- Minimum 3 sections per article
- Internal links to related LENGOLF pages required (especially /golf, /golf-club-rental, /golf-in-thailand-guide)

## Keyword Density
- Primary keyword: aim for 1–2% density (not stuffed)
- LSI/related keywords should appear naturally
- Avoid over-optimising — write for humans first

## E-E-A-T Signals (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
- Include specific facts and numbers (green fees, distances, times) — not vague generalities
- Cite sources for regulatory/legal claims (visa rules, baggage policies)
- Add "last updated" or publication date context where relevant
- Avoid absolute superlatives without qualification ("the best course in Asia" needs context)

## Legal / Liability Red Flags
- Visa and entry requirements: ALWAYS add a disclaimer that requirements change and readers should verify with official Thai immigration sources
- Airline baggage fees: ALWAYS note that fees change and readers should check directly with their airline
- Prices: Add "as of [date]" qualifier on any specific price claim
- Health advice (heat, fitness): Be general, not prescriptive; don't give medical advice

## Internal Linking Targets (always try to link to these where relevant)
- `/golf` — LENGOLF main page
- `/golf-club-rental` — club rental page
- `/golf-in-thailand-guide` — hub page
- `/guide/what-is-a-golf-simulator` — simulator explainer
- `/cost/golf-simulator-prices-bangkok` — pricing page
