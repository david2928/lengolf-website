## 2. Verification Results

### C-01 — Standard tip 200–400 THB

**Status: REVISED**

Multiple 2025–2026 sources (fairwaysofeden.com, asiagolfjourney.com, birdie.in.th, golfsavers.com) consistently show the current baseline is **300–400 THB at budget/public courses** and **400–500 THB at mid-range courses**. The knowledge base figure of 200–400 THB is below current market rates. Article updated to 300–400 THB (public) and 400–500 THB (mid-range).

### C-02 — Premium courses 400–500 THB

**Status: REVISED**

Sources show premium/resort courses now command **500–600 THB** as the expected baseline, with exceptional service at 700–1,000 THB. The knowledge base ceiling of 500 THB is below current expectations. Article updated.

### C-03 — Mandatory caddie fee 100–200 THB

**Status: REVISED — significant discrepancy**

This is the largest deviation. Sources consistently place the mandatory caddie fee at **400–600 THB**, not 100–200 THB. The knowledge base figure appears to be significantly out of date. Article updated to 400–600 THB with a note that the total caddie-related cost per round (fee + tip) is typically 900–1,400 THB. **This figure should be flagged for human review before publishing**, as it contradicts the knowledge base by 3–4x.

### C-04 — Tip handed directly to caddie at 18th green

**Status: CONFIRMED** — Consistent across all sources reviewed.

### C-05 — Each golfer in a group tips separately

**Status: CONFIRMED** — Standard practice described across multiple sources.

### C-06 — Exceptional service 500 THB+

**Status: REVISED**

Given the upward shift in baseline tips, exceptional service now warrants **700–1,000 THB**. Article updated accordingly.

### C-07 — LENGOLF is an indoor simulator

**Status: NOT INDEPENDENTLY VERIFIED via web search** — treated as brand fact provided by the client. No changes needed.
