# Stage 3: Review (You)

**Who does this:** You

## What to read

Open the two files Claude created:

1. `content/2.pending-review/<slug>.report.md` — the verification summary
2. `content/2.pending-review/<slug>.md` — the article itself

## Your options

**Happy with it?** Tell Claude:
> "Approve `best-golf-simulators-bangkok`"

Claude will:
1. Move the article to `content/3.approved/<slug>.md`
2. Move the report to `content/old-reports/<slug>.report.md` (audit trail)
3. Run Stage 4 (publish to data files)

**Want changes?** Tell Claude:
> "Fix the disputed claim about caddie fees in `best-golf-simulators-bangkok`"
> "The green fee for Alpine Golf Club is wrong — it's 2,200 THB on weekdays"

Claude will update the article and re-verify, then give you a new report.

**Want to skip a flag?** Tell Claude:
> "Ignore the disclaimer flag on airline fees in this article — we'll add a general disclaimer in the footer instead"

## What NOT to do

Don't manually edit the article and move it yourself — tell Claude what to change so it can update the report and re-verify if needed.
